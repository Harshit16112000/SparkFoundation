Can I use any branch across India to operate my account?

Answer

Yes, you can access and operate your account from any branch of IDBI Bank across India. You can electronically transfer funds from one account to another account in any branch across cities. You can withdraw and deposit cash upto Rs. 50,000 per day at any branch.

Do I get, International Debit cum ATM Card, SMS Banking, Internet Banking, and PhoneBanking?

Answer

Yes. These services have been introduced to make banking a convenient and a hassle-free option for you. The International Debit cum ATM Card has been packed with additional features to convert it to a virtual bank.

What is the withdrawal limit on my International Debit cum ATM Card?

Answer

You can withdraw upto Rs. 25,000 per day from your International Debit cum ATM card. You can also use your International Debit cum ATM card at other bank ATM.

What is the maximum amount that can be received in cash by the beneficiary

Answer

The maximum amount that can be received in cash is Rs. 50,000. In case the amount of remittance exceeds Rs. 50,000, the full amount will be paid by way of Pay Order or by directly crediting the account of the beneficiary (if account is maintained with the Bank's branch).
